At Almond Yachts, we strive for excellence. We’re inspired by nature and the human spirit to explore. In nature, the Almond hull and shell protects its payload through the harshest of conditions. Using high-tech composite engineering, Almond Yachts are designed to protect, and provide an engaging, hassle free yachting experience.
Almond Yachts is dedicated to sustainable boating. This is why all Almond Yachts are designed for electric propulsion only. The time has come to make the change. So we’re starting with a clean page and clean electric motors.
